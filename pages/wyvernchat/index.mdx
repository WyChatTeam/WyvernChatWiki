## What is WyvernChat?
WyvernChat aims to be an AI Swiss Army knife, providing both content for the usage of AI technologies and the platform utilize such content. WyvernChat is a web-based application that allows you to chat with AI models, create stories, and scenarios, and even play text-based games. WyvernChat is designed to be a one-stop-shop for all your AI needs, and we're always looking to expand our offerings. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please let us know on our [Discord](https://discord.gg/xs8v3TbwJq) server.
